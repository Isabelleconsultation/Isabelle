About Me

Since childhood, a deep spiritual awareness has gently guided my path, shaping my life through powerful inner experiences that have left a lasting impression on my soul. These moments of profound connection continue to inspire my work to this day.

At the age of 17, I discovered Astrology—not as a mere tool for prediction, but as a key to understanding the subtle link between the Microcosm and the Macrocosm. This realization awakened in me a profound sense of gratitude and unity with the greater cosmic order.

Driven by this insight, I have dedicated over three decades to the study and integration of psychological, spiritual, and esoteric disciplines. My work is grounded in both experience and formal training. I hold certifications in Astrology (British Astrological and Psychic Society), Graphology (Société Française de Graphologie), and Morphopsychology (Société Française de Morphopsychologie)—an approach that deciphers psychological patterns through the evolving shapes of the body and face.

Meditation has also been a daily practice for many years. Two decades ago, my mediumistic sensitivity naturally emerged, allowing me to connect with two spiritual guides who accompany me during meditative states. They offer clarity and insight on philosophical and existential questions, always bringing me back to the intuitive wisdom we all carry within—the quiet voice of the higher Self.

At the heart of my vocation is a sincere love for humanity.

I approach each encounter with heartfelt interest, inspired by the richness of every individual journey. My aspiration is to place my knowledge and experience in service of others.
